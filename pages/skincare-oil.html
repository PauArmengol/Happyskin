Facial routine for oily skin

Oily skin types have an excess of sebum that needs to be regulated, as it is the cause of the appearance of small pimples or acne.


Oily skin is characterized by the presence of oil generally located throughout the face.
In combination skin, oil tends to be more concentrated in the well-known T-zone (forehead, nose, and chin), while in oily skin, having oil evenly distributed on the face is the usual pattern.

Here are the three most important points that every oily skin should always keep in mind in their beauty routine:

Double facial cleansing. Facial cleansing should be more intensive for oily skin, as it is important to remove excess oil and impurities that are oil-based. Therefore, the use of an oil-based cleanser is crucial for this skin type.

Exfoliation. A deeper cleansing than the previous step that focuses on unclogging pores and removing dead skin cells.

Hydration. Keeping the skin well-hydrated is essential to achieve the optimal balance of oil and moisture in the skin. This will help regulate the secretion of oil that originates from your skin.
